Why Choose Navon Jobs Nepal?

1. Decades of Global Expertise

Navon Jobs is part of a global network with offices in Romania, UAE, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Nepal. Founded by Georgiana Mart, a visionary entrepreneur with a strong background in law, journalism, and fashion, Navon Jobs brings a wealth of experience in international recruitment. Georgiana Mart’s innovative approach and deep understanding of global markets have helped thousands of workers, like you, secure promising careers abroad.

Our Nepal branch draws from the strengths of our Romania-based headquarters, offering the same professionalism, dedication, and transparency that have made Navon Jobs a trusted name in recruitment globally. With our expertise, you can trust that we will match you with the right employer, negotiate fair wages, and ensure your well-being.

2. Comprehensive Support from Day One

We don’t just find you a job — we guide you through the entire process. From preparing your paperwork to offering pre-departure orientation, we make sure that you are ready to start your new job in Europe without hassle. With Navon Jobs Nepal, you’ll receive:

  • Visa and Work Permit Assistance: We handle the paperwork so that you can focus on preparing for your new adventure.
  • Pre-Departure Training: We equip you with cultural and language training through our sister company, Navon Global Languages Institute (NGLI). This ensures that you feel confident and prepared to thrive in a new country.
  • On-the-Job Support: Even after you start your new job in Europe, our team remains available to assist with any issues or concerns.

3. Wide Range of Jobs for All Skill Levels

Navon Jobs Nepal offers positions for:

  • Skilled Workers: Engineers, IT professionals, carpenters, electricians, welders, and more.
  • Semi-Skilled Workers: Factory workers, machine operators, truck drivers, and construction workers.
  • Unskilled Workers: General laborers, cleaners, warehouse staff, and helpers.

Whatever your skill level, we have an opportunity for you. We partner with reputable companies across Europe that offer competitive salaries, safe working conditions, and long-term career growth.

4. Transparency and Trust

At Navon Jobs Nepal, we operate with honesty and transparency, which sets us apart from other recruitment agencies. You will receive clear information about job details, contract terms, salaries, and working conditions before you even leave Nepal. We take pride in zero hidden fees and no false promises — what you see is what you get.

5. Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. We partner only with companies that provide safe working conditions and comply with European labor standards. We also offer insurance and other safety nets to protect you during your employment abroad.

6. What Makes Navon Jobs Nepal Different?

Unlike many agencies that promise the world but fail to deliver, Navon Jobs is deeply committed to the well-being and success of our workers. Our approach to recruitment is not just about filling vacancies — it’s about creating long-term partnerships between workers and employers.

Emilia Foundation

Our Story

We strongly believe that giving enriches you, and anyone can offer a smile, a kind word, love, compassion, or understanding – a piece of your heart! Every day we receive gifts that we should appreciate: a new day of life, a new sunrise, the clear blue sky, the beautiful nature that delights our eyes and lifts our spirits, and the pure smile of a child. Let us give, by loving, and love, by giving! Only in this way will we understand the good that a simple smile can bring.

If you want to advance on the path of knowledge, gift a book; if you want to have an enlightened heart, offer comfort to those in need; if you want to know freedom, buy a dove from the market and set it free to fly towards the sky.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring prosperity to families and the Neamț community, to support young people in discovering their vocations through educational, cultural, and artistic programs, and to help those in need lead a decent life, because the well-being of each individual depends on the common good.

Our Vision

In our vision, every person can contribute to a better world with a smile or a kind word. We believe that only through mutual support can we have a united community based on healthy values, and only then can we live in peace and harmony.

Our Values

Our values: generosity, respect, involvement, assistance, Christianity, altruism, gratitude, generosity.

Our Goal

Our goal is to initiate social programs and cultural and educational events to create a favorable environment for the development of children and families from disadvantaged backgrounds.

The Emilia Foundation operates in the localities of Neamț County, which has over 446,000 inhabitants. Many families in the county live on the edge of existence, children have difficult access to education and other extracurricular school programs.
